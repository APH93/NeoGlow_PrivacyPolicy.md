Welcome to the NeoGlow support page. We are committed to providing a high-quality experience for our users while maintaining absolute privacy.

🛠 Frequently Asked Questions
1. Does NeoGlow require an account?
No. NeoGlow is a "buy once, use forever" app. There are no accounts to create, no passwords to remember, and I do not collect your email address.

2. How do I restore my purchase?
Since NeoGlow is a paid app on the Mac App Store, your purchase is linked to your Apple ID. If you switch to a new Mac, simply log into the Mac App Store with the same Apple ID, and you can download NeoGlow again for free.

3. Where is my data stored?
All settings, configurations, and assets used within NeoGlow are stored locally on your Mac. I do not have servers, and I never see your files.

4. Does the app work offline?
Yes. NeoGlow does not require an internet connection to function, as it does not communicate with any external servers.

If you encounter a bug or have a feature request, we want to hear from you! Since we don't track your usage, user feedback is our only way to improve the app.please leave a review as I typically respond within 24–48 hours.
