# NeoGlow

A focused keyboard light for MacBook Neo.

NeoGlow helps you see your keyboard in low light by projecting a controlled light bar from the lower edge of the screen toward the keys.

## What It Does

Instead of flooding the whole display with brightness, NeoGlow uses Photon Steering to direct light where it is actually useful for typing.

## Features

- Focused screen-edge keyboard illumination
- Fast calibration around the `ASDF` row
- Controls for angle, warmth, darkness, and boost
- Menu bar access for quick control
- Cursor-aware cutout to reduce glare near the pointer

## How It Works

1. Open NeoGlow
2. Press `Engage`
3. Place your left hand nails on `ASDF`
4. Adjust the light until the keys are comfortably visible

## Best Experience

NeoGlow works best on the main desktop. Some fullscreen apps may block the projection because of normal macOS windowing behavior.

## Privacy

NeoGlow does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

## Availability

Download on the Mac App Store.
